Frequently Asked Questions

Here, we answer your most popularly asked questions about The PriceMarketplace

What is The PriceMarketplace? +
The PriceMarketplace is Nigeria's only crowd-sourced, AI-powered foodstuff price collection and aggregation dashboard.
How does The Pricemarketplace work? +
Our platform simply allows foodstuff buyers and sellers to login and update the prices of staple foods in their locality, providing valuable information to other market goers and also allowing much needed government regulation and oversight.
How do I Contribute? +
If you're majorly a buyer/consumer, you can contribute by hitting the "Add Prices" button above and help update foodstuff prices in your locality so others can see them. If you're a foodstuff vendor or shop/store owner, you can also do the same to update the prices of the foodstuff items you have in stock. Additionally, vendors or sellers can also hit the "partner-with-us" button to get their physical stores or shops registered and displayed on the platform.
